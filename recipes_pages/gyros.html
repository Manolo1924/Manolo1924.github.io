Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, diced cucumber, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt. Mix well to make Tzatziki sauce. Refrigerate it while you prepare the rest of the gyro.
  2. In a separate bowl, marinate the thinly sliced lamb or chicken with olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Allow it to sit for at least 30 minutes to soak up the flavors.
  3. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated meat until it's nicely browned and fully cooked.
  4. Warm the pita bread rounds on the grill or in the oven.
  5. Now it's time to assemble the gyros. Place some of the cooked meat on each pita bread round.
  6. Top the meat with Tzatziki sauce, sliced tomatoes, and red onions.
  7. Wrap the pita around the filling, securing it with foil or parchment paper to hold it together.
  8. Your Greek Gyros are ready to be served. Enjoy!
