Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Squeeze excess liquid from the thawed spinach and place it in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add the crumbled feta cheese, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dill, and parsley to the spinach. Mix well.
  4. Brush a 9x13-inch baking dish with melted butter.
  5. Lay one sheet of phyllo dough in the dish and brush with more melted butter. Repeat this process, layering several sheets until the bottom is covered.
  6. Spoon the spinach and cheese mixture over the phyllo layers and spread it evenly.
  7. Layer more phyllo sheets on top, brushing each with melted butter.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown.
