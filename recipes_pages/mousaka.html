Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Brush the eggplant slices with olive oil and roast them in the oven until tender. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground meat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes, red wine, parsley, and cinnamon. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the roasted eggplant and meat mixture. Repeat until the dish is filled.
  6. Combine the Parmesan cheese and breadcrumbs and sprinkle on top.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown. Let it cool slightly before serving.
